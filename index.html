About Us!

Science Olympiad, as the name suggests is a competition for students to compete in fields of engineering, sciences, math, and more!
At Beckman, we strive to give the resources for each and every member to succeed in their events.

Our mission is to host exciting competitive tournaments and professional development workshops
that showcase innovative STEM content to students and teachers throughout the United States.
