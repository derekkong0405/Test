About Us!

Science Olympiad, as the name suggests, is a competition for students to bridge technical and theoretical work by competing in engineering, science, and math events!
At Beckman, we strive to provide the resources for each and every member to feel the magic of science by getting their hands into concepts, not just reading about them.

Our mission is to foster a supportive learning environment and a tight-knit community of scientists and
engineers who are eager to take advantage of resources that will help them explore STEM beyond the classroom
